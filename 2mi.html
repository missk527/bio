Key Notes

Units of Length and Powers of Ten
  • Base unit: meter (m).
  • Kilometer (km): 10³ m = 1,000 m
  • Millimeter (mm): 10⁻³ m = 0.001 m
  • Micrometer (μm): 10⁻⁶ m
  • Nanometer (nm): 10⁻⁹ m
  • Exception: centimeter (cm) = 10⁻² m = 10 mm
Human height: ~1.5–2 m Tallest building: ~1 km Ant: a few mm Eukaryotic cell: ~10 μm Virus: ~100 nm Atom: <1 nm
Resolution and Microscopes
  • Resolution: shortest distance between two points still seen as separate.
  • Light microscopes: ~0.2 μm resolution (wavelength of light); show whole cells but not fine organelles.
  • Electron microscopes: ~0.1 nm (wavelength of electrons); ~2000× finer detail.

Magnification without resolution just makes a bigger blur—like zooming into a low-quality photo.

Magnification
  • Formula: Magnification = Image size ÷ Real size
  • Rule: convert to the same unit before calculating.
  • Example: 30 μm cell appears 30 mm → 30 mm ÷ 30 μm = 1000×
